Minor Website Updates Checklist

Each minor update is ₹100.
Package of 10 updates: ₹1,000.

1️⃣ Text Updates

Change page titles or headings
Edit paragraph content
Correct typos or grammar
Update quotes, testimonials, or bios
Update captions under images or videos
Update meta descriptions

2️⃣ Images & Media

Replace or add images
Resize or compress images
Update logos or icons
Replace background images
Add/remove banners or sliders
Replace embedded videos or media
Update downloadable files (PDFs, docs, etc.)

3️⃣ Links & Navigation

Update internal page links
Add/remove external links
Fix broken links
Update menu items or navigation labels

4️⃣ Design & Styling (CSS)

Change fonts, colors, or text styles
Adjust spacing, margins, or padding
Update button styles or hover effects
Minor layout tweaks (alignments, columns, images)
Update background colors or gradients

5️⃣ Contact & Forms

Update email addresses or phone numbers
Add/remove form fields (minor)
Change form submission email or URL
Update embedded maps

6️⃣ Social Media & Integration

Update social media icons/links
Add/remove share buttons
Update embedded feeds (Instagram, Twitter, YouTube)

7️⃣ Content Blocks / Sections

Add/remove small sections (text or images)
Reorder sections on a page
Update lists, tables, or bullet points

8️⃣ SEO & Metadata

Update page titles and meta descriptions
Update alt text for images
Add header tags (H1, H2)
Fix minor schema markup

9️⃣ Other Minor Fixes

Fix small HTML errors
Correct broken images or files
Adjust responsiveness on mobile devices
Minor animation or hover effect changes

⚡ Notes:

Each minor update = ₹100.
10 updates package = ₹1,000.
Updates must fall under the items listed above.
Anything outside this list (new pages, major redesign, new features) will be quoted separately.
